Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

To sign up as a volunteer, visit the "Sign Up" page, fill in your details, and submit the form. You'll receive an email confirmation once your registration is complete.

If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You will receive a reset link via email to create a new password.

Event Questions

Only admins can create events. If you are an admin, navigate to the "Event Creation" page, fill out the event details, and click "Submit" to create the event.

To sign up for an event, go to the "Events" page, browse available events, and click "Sign Up" on the event you'd like to participate in.

Volunteer Questions

You can view your total volunteer hours on your profile page. The hours are automatically tracked based on your participation in events.

To update your profile information, go to your profile page, click "Edit Profile," make your changes, and save the updates.
